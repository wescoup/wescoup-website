Tony’s Pickleball Tracking Instructions

Core Tracking

  • Return of Serve Tracking is based on the return of serve. For every serve, log whether the returner won or lost the point. The tracker will automatically update the score based on this data.
  • Undo Last Point Made a mistake? This button will remove the very last action you recorded, including the score change.

Singles-Specific Tracking

  • Unforced Errors & Third Shot Misses Use these buttons to log unforced errors and third shot misses. The tallies for these are automatically reset at the start of each new game.
  • Dynamic Scoring The game score is updated dynamically as you track points.

Doubles-Specific Tracking

Doubles tracking is a future feature.
